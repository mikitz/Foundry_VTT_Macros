MAGICAL STORM

Type: Abjuration Storm

Description: A storm infused with abjuration magic repels ships, as if protecting something in the sea.

Duration: [[1d24]] hours.

Effect: Right before the storm ends, a ship within it is teleported [[5d20]] miles in a random direction.

MAGICAL STORM

Type: Conjuration Storm

Description: When conjuration magic churns within a storm's heart, creatures of wind and rain emerge from distant realms to attack anything they encounter.

Duration: [[1d24]] hours.

Effect: The ship encounters [[1d3]] hostile air elementals.

MAGICAL STORM

Type: Divination Storm

Description: When divination magic seeps into a storm, howling winds and residual whispers temporarily scramble the knowledge of a ship's crew

Duration: [[1d24]] hours.

Effect: For [[1d3]] days, the ship's crew suffers disadvantage on all quality checks.

MAGICAL STORM

Type: Enchantment Storm

Description: Storms infused with enchantment magic disarm a creature's sense of danger, enthralling mariners and causing them to speed into danger.

Duration: [[1d24]] hours.

Effect: During the storm, any checks made to resolve hazards or manage the ship are made with disadvantage.

MAGICAL STORM

Type: Evocation Storm

Description: These ferocious storms are wracked with thunderbolts, driving sheets of acid rain, exploding meteors, and other dangers.

Duration: [[1d24]] hours.

Effect: After each hour of the storm, every component aboard the ship takes [[1d10]] fire, [[1d10]] acid, and [[1d10]] lightning damage. In addition, roll a [[1d20]] and add the crew's quality. On a 10 or less, the crew's quality decreases by 1, and [[1d6]] members of the crew are killed.

MAGICAL STORM

Type: Illusion Storm

Description: A storm brimming with illusions poses little direct harm to a ship, but experienced navigators know it poses an insidious threat.

Duration: [[1d24]] hours.

Effect: During the storm, the ship travels in a random direction that isn't its intended course.

MAGICAL STORM

Type: Necromancy Storm

Description: Ghosts howl and whirl in this storm's wind, while the remains of long-dead mariners stir in their watery graves.

Duration: [[1d24]] hours.

Effect: During the storm, [[1d4]] specters, [[2d4]] ghouls, and [[4d6]] zombies emerge from the waves to attack the ship.

MAGICAL STORM

Type: Transmutation Storm

Description: These violently unpredictable storms cause the waves to warp and twist. The storm transforms the water around the ship into an amalgamation of ice, stone, and stranger materials, making travel difficult and damaging the ship.

Duration: [[1d24]] hours.

Effect: The ship's hull takes [[4d10]] bludgeoning damage, and the ship's speed decreases by half during the storm.

STORM

Type: Heavy Gale

Difficulty Class: DC 10

CHECKS

The Captain: Intelligence (water vehicles)

First Mate: Charisma (Persuasion)

Bosun: Strength (carpenter's tools)

Surgeon: Intelligence (Medicine)

Quartermaster: Wisdom (Nature)

Crew: [[1d20]]

CHECK RESULTS

Total Success: The ship survives unscathed. The crew's quality score increases by 1 for 1d4 days.

Success: The ship survives unscathed.

Failure: The ship's components each take [[4d10]] bludgeoning damage. The crew's quality score decreases by 1. The ship struggles, moving at half speed that day.

Total Failure: The ship's components each take [[10d10]] bludgeoning damage. The crew's quality score decreases by 2, and 10 percent of the crew is washed overboard and lost. The ship is blown off course and struggles to recover its bearings, moving in a random direction.
