ELDRITCH MIST

Type: Ghost Fog

Difficulty Class: DC 15 Intelligence (Arcana) check to recognize it as an eldritch mist

Description: The souls of the dead want the living to join them.

Effects: Every hour a ship is immersed in ghost fog, roll any die. On an even number, [[2d4]] hostile specters appear and attack those on the ship.

If a humanoid creature dies in ghost fog, its spirit rises as a specter that is hostile toward all creatures that aren't undead.

All non-undead creatures immersed in ghost fog are vulnerable to necrotic damage.

ELDRITCH MIST

Type: Shadowfell Fog

Difficulty Class: DC 15 Intelligence (Arcana) check to recognize it as an eldritch mist

Description: When a vessel travels through this oppressive, shadow-haunted miasma, its crew and passengers feel despair.

Effects: Each hour a vessel is immersed in the fog, the crew must make a quality score check by rolling a [[1d20]] and adding their quality score to the number. The DC for this check is 10 + the number of hours the ship has been immersed in the Shadowfell fog.

If the crew fails this check, their quality score decreases by 1 while within the Shadowfell fog and for 1 day afterward.

ELDRITCH MIST

Type: Wild Magic Fog

Difficulty Class: DC 15 Intelligence (Arcana) check to recognize it as an eldritch mist

Description: Few know the origins of wild magic fog, with some claiming it is the creation of capricious gods while others believe it is the result of magical experimentation gone wrong.

Effects: Whenever a creature in wild magic fog casts a spell of 1st level or higher, roll on the Wild Magic Surge table in chapter 3, "Classes," of the Player's Handbook to create a magical effect.
